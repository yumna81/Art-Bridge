Who We Are

Connecting Art, Artists, and the People Who Love Them

ArtBridge started with a simple idea: talented local artists deserve an easier way to reach people who genuinely want custom, handmade artwork.

Since launching, we've grown into a community of hundreds of verified artists across Indonesia, spanning digital illustration, traditional painting, and handmade craft.

Our mission is to make commissioning art as simple as browsing a portfolio, chatting with the artist, and placing an order — while helping artists turn their passion into real, sustainable income.
